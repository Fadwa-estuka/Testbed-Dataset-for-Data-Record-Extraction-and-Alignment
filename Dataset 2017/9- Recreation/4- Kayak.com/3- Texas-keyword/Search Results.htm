    Battleship USS Texas and San Jacinto Monument tour

    (No reviews)
    Duration: 6h 30m
    On this tour guided by a Certified Tourism Ambassador, you'll go on board the "USS Texas," once considered the most dangerous weapon on Earth.  It was armed with over 70 high caliber weapons, with some of it's artillery capable of hitting targets up to 12 miles away leaving a hole 10 feet deep and the size of a basketball field! You'll see how these brave men lived life while on the high seas from Okinawa to Iwo Jima and hear the amazing journey of battleship "Texas."  Afterwards you'll visit the tallest stone memorial in the world, the San Jacinto Monument, which stands 570 ft tall.  You'll also visit the San Jacinto Battleground, where an army led by Sam Houston defeated Santa Anna for independence from Mexico. You'll visit the actual battle site as well as the San Jacinto Museum that sits on 1,200 acres of meadow. You'll tour in a climate controlled vehicle that includes complimentary bottled water, snacks and hotel pickup from inside the central Houston area.

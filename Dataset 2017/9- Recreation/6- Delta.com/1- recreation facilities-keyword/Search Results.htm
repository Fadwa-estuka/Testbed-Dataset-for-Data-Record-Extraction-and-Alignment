Search Tips
If you're having trouble finding the information you are looking for, here are some tips to make your search more successful.
  • Make your search as specific as possible. Searching for "business class London" will return more relevant results than "good service England."
  • There is no need to include "and" between your search terms. By default, we will only return pages that include all of your terms.
  • Keep in mind that the order in which you enter your search terms will affect your search results.
  • Common words such as "where" and "how," and certain single letters or digits will be ignored. if you want to include these words in your search, you can put a "+" in front of them or put quotation marks around two or more words (for example, "where are you").
  • Search results are not case sensitive. Searching for "new york," "New York," or "NEW YORK" will all return the same results.

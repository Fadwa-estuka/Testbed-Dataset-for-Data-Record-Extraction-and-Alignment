ICICI Bank Answers

I have a business checking account. Can I send money to India using e-Transfer (ACSS) from this account?

e-Transfer (ACSS) can only be used to send money from a consumer checking or savings account. You cannot use a business or a commercial account to send money to India using e-Transfer (ACSS). This is not allowed as per e-Transfer (ACSS) rules and regulations. Read More
